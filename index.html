What is a Meta Display AI assistant?

A Meta Display AI assistant appears directly on smart glasses like Meta Ray‑Ban Display, showing step‑by‑step instructions, notifications, and short responses in‑lens.

Meta AI focuses on visual answers, navigation, translation, and quick commands on the device itself.

Super complements this by acting as the external action layer — controlling browsers, apps, and devices that glasses alone cannot.

Hands‑free workflow with Super + Meta Display

  1. Pair Meta Display glasses with Super using the Meta Display setup flow in Super’s settings. Official setup guide.
  2. Speak or gesture: “Super, check my calendar and prep directions.”
  3. Super operates a real browser session, gathers the info, and streams summaries back to your display.
  4. Repeated tasks reuse the same computer-use cache, so the workflow improves instead of restarting from scratch.
